PixelPal

A mobile app for movie lovers – built with Flutter & Firebase.

Overview

PixelPal is a mobile application developed as part of the Orbital program at the National University of Singapore. It helps movie lovers discover films, connect with friends, and keep track of movies they’ve watched. PixelPal also digitizes physical tickets with Google ML text recognition and provides personalized recommendations.

View GitHub repo →

Features

  • Discover and search for movies you love
  • Connect with friends who share your interests
  • Digital ticket storage with auto text recognition
  • Personalized recommendations based on history

Technologies

  • Flutter (cross-platform UI)
  • Dart
  • Firebase (auth, real-time DB, cloud storage)
